Refund Policy

1. Introduction

The refund policy of the DODGE-Gym Platform aims to ensure a fair and transparent user experience while protecting user rights for refunds of points or services under specified conditions in line with the platform’s regulations.

2. General Refund Conditions

2.1. Non-Refundable Cases:

  • Points Packages:
    • Purchased points packages within the platform are non-refundable.
    • Services that have been initiated:
      • No refunds are allowed for services whose status has changed from “New” to any other status (e.g., “In Progress” or “Completed”).
      • This applies regardless of whether the service was purchased using points or through the platform’s payment gateway.
  • Savings Packages:
    • Special offer savings packages provided by the platform are non-refundable.

2.2. Refundable Cases:

  • Services in “New” Status:
    • Services that remain in the “New” status and have not yet been initiated are eligible for refunds.
    • This applies whether the service was purchased using points or through the payment gateway.

2.3. Platform’s Right to Cancel Orders:

  • The platform reserves the right to cancel any service request for any reason (e.g., technical issues or circumstances beyond control).
  • Points or funds for the canceled service will be refunded to the user’s wallet within the platform.
  • The user will be notified clearly and transparently about the reason for the cancellation.

3. Refund Process

3.1. How to Refund Eligible Services:

  • For services in the “New” status or canceled by the platform:
    • The refunded points or funds will be added to the user’s wallet within the platform.
    • The user can utilize the refunded points or funds for future purchases on the platform.

3.2. Additional Conditions:

  • Refunded points or funds cannot be withdrawn as cash outside the platform.
  • Refunded points or funds can only be used within the platform to acquire new services.

4. Policy Amendments

  • The platform reserves the right to modify the refund policy at any time to ensure compliance with legal changes or to enhance user experience.
  • Users will be notified of any significant changes before they take effect.
